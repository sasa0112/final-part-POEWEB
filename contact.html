Order & Consultation Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. If you need to cancel an order or consultation, please follow the steps below:

  • Order Cancellations: Must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled pickup or delivery time.
  • Consultation Cancellations: Please notify us at least 12 hours in advance to reschedule or receive a full refund.
  • How to Cancel: Call us at +27 11 123 4567 or email cancel@thebreadbasket.co.za with your order or booking details.
  • Late Cancellations: May incur a small service charge to cover preparation costs.

We appreciate your understanding and continued support as we strive to make healthy baking simple and enjoyable for everyone.
